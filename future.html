Future Projects

This fall, I have been working on a purple heart sweater using a pattern from OlivaMadeShop. This pattern is the most complex pattern I have ever worked on. Besides the sheer size of the project, the crochet pattern changes colors every couple of rows. In the past, I have struggled with changing yarn colors. However, due to working on this sweater, I have become a master at switching yarn colors. I will surely have a lot of ends to sew in when I finish! Due to a busier schedule, I have had less time to crochet compared to my previous semesters. I have still been making steady progress on the sweater. I hope to finish this sweater by the end of the semester.

I am interested in starting to incorporate my environmentalism into my crocheting hobby through creating earth-inspired crochet items. I want to create traditional blue and green earths as well as pop-art inspired earths with a variety of different colors. I picture incorporating these earths into pillows or wall decorations. In order to fully pursue this concept, I envision myself using used fibers from local second-hand stores. The textile industry is one of the most polluting industries and I would love to diverge fibers away from the landfill.
